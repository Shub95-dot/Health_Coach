### 🩹 Complete Injury Management ✅

**Smart Classification:**
- ✅ **RED FLAG** (Medical referral): Fractures, tears, severe injuries
- ✅ **YELLOW FLAG** (Modify training): Tendonitis, strains, chronic pain
- ✅ **GREEN FLAG** (Safe to train): Minor soreness, no issues

**Automatic Exercise Modification:**
- ✅ Identifies problematic exercises for each injury
- ✅ Removes risky movements automatically
- ✅ Adds safe, effective alternatives
- ✅ Maintains workout quality despite limitations

**Example:**
```
User: "I have knee tendonitis"

Bot: "Got it - knee tendonitis. We can handle this smartly!

I'll modify your program to:
✓ Skip problematic exercises (squats, lunges, running)
✓ Add knee-friendly movements (RDLs, hip thrusts, cycling)
✓ Still get you amazing results

Rule: Any sharp pain = stop immediately. We train smart, not stupid! 😉"
```

### Example 2: Injury Management
```
User: I need a program but I have a bad shoulder

Bot: I hear you about the shoulder. Your safety is my top priority. Let me ask a few questions:

• Where exactly does it hurt?
• How would you rate the pain (1-10)?
• Was this diagnosed by a doctor?
• When did it start?

User: Front of shoulder, about 5/10, no diagnosis, been hurting for 2 weeks

Bot: Alright, so it sounds like possible shoulder impingement - we can definitely work around this! 👍

Here's the plan:
✅ I'll remove exercises that stress your shoulder
✅ I'll include safe alternatives
✅ We'll focus on what you CAN do
✅ You'll still see great results!

But listen - if the pain gets worse, you stop and see a doctor. Deal?

User: Deal. I want to build muscle, 12 weeks, at the gym

Bot: Perfect! Let me put together your custom 12-week plan for muscle gain... This is gonna be good! 💪

[Generates shoulder-safe program]

🩹 I've modified your program to protect your shoulder:

❌ Removed 5 exercises that could aggravate your injury:
   • Overhead press
   • Heavy bench press
   • Dips
   • Upright row
   • Military press

✅ Added 5 safe, effective alternatives:
   • Landmine press
   • Cable chest press (neutral grip)
   • Face pull
   • Lateral raise (light)
   • Push-ups (controlled)

💪 You'll still make great progress while protecting your shoulder!

=== YOUR 12-WEEK MUSCLE-BUILDING PROGRAM ===
...

Let's do this! 🔥
```
